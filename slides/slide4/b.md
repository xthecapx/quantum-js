### The rules

1. Ale and Brayan need to figure out how to beat the Referee.
2. The Referee will ask a one-bit question, such as 0 or 1.
3. These are the winning conditions

| x, y | win | lose | 
|---|---|---|
| (0, 0) | a === b | a !== b |
| (0, 1) | a === b | a !== b |
| (1, 0) | a === b | a !== b |
| (1, 1) | a !== b | a === b |
